Shipping & Delivery Policy

1. Shipping Timeline

All orders are processed within 24-48 hours. Estimated delivery time:

  • Metro Cities: 3-5 Business Days
  • Rest of India: 5-7 Business Days

2. Shipping Charges

We offer FREE Shipping on all prepaid orders across India.

3. Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an SMS/WhatsApp with your tracking ID from our courier partners (Delhivery/BlueDart/Shiprocket).

4. Cash on Delivery (COD)

COD is available for most pin codes. Please ensure someone is available to receive the package and pay the exact amount at the time of delivery.
